About Us

Checked It, an aspiring AI-based start-up, works to revolutionize the healthcare industry with its Advance AI toolkit. We aim to help the utmost people in need irrespective of their background by providing our reliable diagnosis and pre-diagnosis services of diseases at an affordable price. With the help of our AI toolkit, an individual would be able to diagnose and pre-diagnose beyond a hundred diseases just with an eye image or a retina image captured from a mobile camera or a fundus camera, respectively.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the healthcare sector with advanced AI tools. It eradicates problems faced by the doctors and general public in the diagnosis and pre-diagnosis of diseases. Furthermore, an AI-powered telemedicine platform helps people check their eye health status. It also aids them through 24/7 availability with an ophthalmologist.
